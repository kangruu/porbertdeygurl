Happy birthday, Aya.

We may not talk as much as we used to, and I might not know every detail of the dreams you are chasing now. Life moves in its own quiet rhythm, carrying people toward different places, different versions of themselves. But no matter how much time passes, I genuinely hope you are happy.

I hope your mornings bring a sense of peace, your days are filled with people who value you, and your heart finds comfort in the things that matter most to you. I hope the goals you've been working toward slowly become reality, and that even on difficult days, you never forget how capable and strong you are.

Even with the quiet distance between us, the memories remain. The late-night conversations, the random moments that somehow became important, the laughter, the lessons, and even the challenges—we shared a chapter of life that I will always carry with gratitude. Some people pass through our lives briefly, while others leave a mark that stays long after the pages have turned. You are one of those people for me.

Talking with you again recently reminded me of something I already knew: time changes many things, but it doesn't erase genuine appreciation. I still look back on those moments fondly, and I am thankful for every memory we created along the way.

And if I'm being honest, I miss you. I miss the conversations, the familiar presence, and the moments we used to share. Life has taken us down different paths, but that doesn't change how much those memories still mean to me.

Thank you for being part of my story, Aya. Thank you for the memories, the kindness, the lessons, and the moments that made that chapter special. I hope this year brings you closer to everything your heart has been searching for, and I hope life continues to be gentle with you.

Take care, always.
